Trends In Fashion

Fashion has always been inspiring people and designers to create something new for the people and set trends from thousands of years from the early Egyptian era to till day and will continue to set trends. Fashion has evolved in different ways accross these time-periods to express themselves and their royalty too. Here's what those trends were about- Ancient Egyptians wore white linen and used makeup. Medieval Europeans wore elaborate clothing. Renaissance people wore fitted clothing. Victorians were modest. The 20th century saw diverse styles.
